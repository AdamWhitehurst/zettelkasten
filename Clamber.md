# Clamber
**_Class:_** *verb*
**_Pronunciation:_** *KLAM-ber*

---

### Definition
To climb, using both feet and hands; climb with effort of difficulty.


### Trivia
From [[Middle English]] *clambren*, possibly a frequentative verb of *climben*.
