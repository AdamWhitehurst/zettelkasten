# Noblesse Oblige
**_Class:_** *noun*
**_Pronunciation:_** *noh-BLESS-uh-BLEEZH*

---

### Definition
The obligation of honorable, generous, and responsible behavior associated with high rank or birth.

### Trivia
In French, literally means "nobility obligates."
